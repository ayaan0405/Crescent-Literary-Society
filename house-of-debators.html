Who We Are

The Art of Argument

The House of Debators is CLS's flagship debate wing — a space where ideas clash, arguments are forged, and the best reasoning wins. We host parliamentary debates, Oxford-style discussions, and inter-college tournaments throughout the academic year.

Members are trained in argumentation structure, rebuttal technique, and the art of impromptu speaking. Whether you're defending a position you believe in or one handed to you by a coin toss — you'll learn to make it stick.

"We don't just argue. We think, we challenge, we refine — until the truth is undeniable."

The House of Debators prepares its members not just for competitions, but for boardrooms, legislatures, and every moment in life where a well-reasoned argument changes the room.

With Inclusion comes Strength — every voice is welcome, every perspective sharpens the debate.

What You'll Learn

Parliamentary Debate

British Parliamentary, Asian Parliamentary, Oxford-style — master multiple formats.

Rebuttal Technique

Dismantle opposing arguments with precision, logic, and calm authority.

Impromptu Speaking

Think fast, speak clearly, and own any room — even without preparation time.

Research & Case-Building

Build airtight arguments from evidence, structure, and compelling narrative.
