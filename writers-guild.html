CLS Wing

Writer's Guild

"Every word is an act of courage."

Where Ink Meets Ideas

Write. Reflect. Publish.

The Writer's Guild is CLS's creative writing and literary publication arm. Members contribute essays, poetry, short fiction, and opinion pieces to Obverse — our internal literary magazine. Regular writing prompts, feedback sessions, and publication drives keep the creative engine running.

We believe every voice deserves a page. Whether you write in whispers or in declarations, the Guild is your home.

"Writing is thinking made visible. We don't wait for permission — we write and then we're heard."

Notable publications: Obverse Magazine, the LitRapture 2K24 Anthology, and the ongoing Unapologetic Campaign — a series celebrating fearless voices.

What We Write

Poetry

From terse imagist lines to sweeping free verse — all forms, all emotions welcome.

Short Fiction

Flash fiction, literary short stories, and experimental narrative prose.

Essays & Opinion

Personal essays, cultural criticism, political commentary — your perspective, fully owned.

Reportage & Review

Book reviews, event coverage, and literary journalism for the Obverse platform.

Our Magazine

Obverse

CLS's flagship literary magazine — publishing the finest writing our members produce. Essays, poetry, fiction, and opinion from voices that refuse to be polite about the world.
