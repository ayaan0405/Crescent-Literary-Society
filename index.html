Who We Are

About Us

The Crescent Literary Society was initiated in the year 2006 with the chief objective to hone the literary skills of the students — with a dash of fun. We aim to enhance leadership qualities and interpersonal communication skills.

A family of self-motivated, creative, confident, adventurous and fearless students with one true mission: to make some unforgettable memories.
