Our Purpose

What We Do

The Crescent Literary Society meets regularly during the academic year to practise debate, creative writing, oratory, and literary discussion. Our members develop the skills to present their views articulately while critiquing opposing positions.

We forge bonds of friendship and intellectual kinship that last a lifetime. With Inclusion comes Strength — our wings welcome every voice, every background, every ambition.

"The Crescent Literary Society was initiated in the year 2006, with the chief objective to hone the literary skills of the students. The community aims to enhance the much-required leadership qualities as well as interpersonal communication skills."
✍️

Creative Expression

Poetry, fiction, essays, and opinion — every voice matters, every story counts.

🎤

Public Speaking

Debate, improv, and oratory sharpen your voice for life beyond the classroom.

🧠

Critical Thinking

Quizzing and debate train you to think fast, think deep, think clearly.

Our Story

History

From a small group of passionate students to a thriving literary community — this is how CLS grew.

2006

Foundation

The Crescent Literary Society is founded with the mission to hone literary skills and celebrate the written and spoken word. A small, passionate group of students start something extraordinary.

2010

Wings Take Shape

CLS expands its activities, formally establishing the House of Debators and the Writer's Guild as distinct wings with dedicated programs and internal competitions.

2014

Improv & Quizzers Join the Family

The Improv wing and Quizzers Circuit are launched, rounding out CLS's four-wing structure and dramatically broadening its reach across the student community.

2018

Obverse Is Born

CLS launches Obverse — its flagship literary magazine — giving student writers a permanent, published platform for their finest work.

2024

LitRapture 2K24

CLS hosts LitRapture 2K24, its biggest annual festival yet — combining debates, improv shows, quiz championships, and literary readings into one unforgettable event.

2025

19 Years Strong

CLS continues to grow, inspire, and include — carrying forward its founding belief: With Inclusion comes Strength.
