Improv

"No script. No limits."

The Stage is Yours

Where Spontaneity Meets Art

Improv at CLS is where performance meets spontaneity. We train members in the core principles of improvisational theatre — "yes, and", active listening, and building scenes in real time. Performances, workshops, and internal shows run throughout the year.

Improv isn't just theatre — it's a life skill. The ability to think fast, adapt, and commit fully to whatever the moment demands is one of the most powerful tools you'll ever develop.

"Yes, and. The two most transformative words in performance — and in life."

Our Improv wing creates a safe, electric, hilariously unpredictable space where confidence is built one scene at a time. No experience required — just a willingness to commit, fail boldly, and laugh at yourself.

Core Improv Principles

🎭

Yes, And

Accept what your scene partner gives you and build on it. Never block, always expand.

👂

Active Listening

The best improv comes from being fully present. Listen, observe, and respond to what's actually there.

🔥

Commit Fully

Half-hearted improv never lands. Throw yourself into the scene — fear is the only enemy.

😂

Make Your Partner Look Good

Great improv is selfless. Lift your scene partner — and they'll lift you.
