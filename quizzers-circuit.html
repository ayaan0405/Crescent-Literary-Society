Quizzers Circuit

"Knowledge is the arena."

For the Brilliantly Obsessed

The Curious Mind's Playground

The Quizzers Circuit brings together the most curious minds in CLS for trivia battles, themed quiz nights, and inter-college competitions. From pop culture to pure academia, no category is off-limits.

Quizzing isn't just about knowing facts � it's about the joy of learning, the thrill of recall, and the electric moment when everything clicks. We compete hard, but we celebrate harder.

"The answer is always there. The art is in knowing where to look � and how fast to hit the buzzer."

Annual event: LitRapture Quiz Championship � our flagship quiz event featuring themed rounds, inter-college battles, and the championship trophy. Open to all CLS members, no prior quizzing experience required.
